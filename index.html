KeRis-Dimas ATAH (Advanced Technology for Agriculture and Health) adalah kelompok riset Universitas Jember yang mengembangkan teknologi maju terjangkau untuk meningkatkan kesejahteraan masyarakat Indonesia. KeRis-Dimas ATAH (Advanced Technology for Agriculture and Health) is a research group of Universitas Jember developing affordable advanced technologies to improve the welfare of Indonesian society.

Fokus Penelitian Utama Main Research Focus

RoadmapResearch Roadmap

Rencana Penelitian 2018–2030 Research Plan 2018–2030

Dari penelitian dasar hingga hilirisasi produk ke pasar. From basic research to product commercialization.

2018 – 2023

Penelitian Dasar (R&D)Basic Research (R&D)

Luaran: produk/teknologi dalam tulisan ilmiah. Fokus: navigasi robot, rehabilitasi, smart farming.Output: scientific publications. Focus: robot navigation, rehabilitation, smart farming.

2022 – 2026

Penelitian TerapanApplied Research

Luaran: jurnal, paten, hak cipta, buku, prototipe. Implementasi nyata sistem navigasi & rehabilitasi.Output: journals, patents, copyright, books, prototypes. Real-world implementation.

2024 – 2028

Penelitian PengembanganDevelopment Research

Luaran: prototipe skala industri, paten. Perbaikan & penyempurnaan teknologi terapan.Output: industrial-scale prototypes, patents. Refinement of applied technology.

2028 – ...

Hilirisasi ke PasarMarket Commercialization

Komersialisasi produk teknologi & pengabdian kepada masyarakat dari hasil riset.Commercialization of technology products & community service from research results.
